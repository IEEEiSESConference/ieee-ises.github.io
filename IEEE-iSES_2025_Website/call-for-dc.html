Call for Design Contest


Download PDF

IEEE-iSES announces a design contest organized by STMicroelectronics. The aim of the contest is to encourage the students to solve real world problems and provide them a platform to showcase their innovations. Whether it is improving the quality of healthcare, allowing better energy efficiency of edge models or integrating AI, all ideas from all background and expertise levels are welcome.

Important Dates

Launch: 17th June, 2024
Last date to receive idea: 7th July, 2024
Orientation session: 20th June and 25th June, 2024, from 7:30 pm to 8:30 pm
Orientation Link: https://shorturl.at/ZfLzX (Orientation deck)
Selected idea announcement and board shipping: 19th July, 2024
Last day of implemented idea submission: 7th November, 2024
