Sponsorship

IEEE International Symposium on Smart Electronic Systems (iSES) 2024, Sponsorship Information

Sponsorship of an international conference represents an organization’s innovations, developments, and capabilities of the world of eminent scientists and engineers who are from academia and industry. The opportunities of IEEE International Symposium on Smart Electronic Systems (iSES) sponsorship are available on a first-come, first-serve basis. So, organizations are advised to reserve the opportunity that spotlights the organization’s name. The following table gives a summary of benefits that possibly an organization can get at IEEE-iSES 2024.

Sponsorship Categories Fees (US $) Sponsorship Privileges from iSES
Silicon 6,000
  1. The sponsor's name would be displayed synonymously with IEEE-iSES 2024.
  2. Appropriately sized logo in the backgrounds and slide templates along with applicable logos
  3. Logo on the conference badge, along with the conference logo.
  4. Registration fee will be waived for up to 10 delegates from the sponsor.
  5. Coverage in electronic media as per conference's publicity plan that gets to be in place.
Gold 4,000
  1. The sponsor would be a co-sponsor and its name would be displayed on every poster of the IEEE-iSES 2024.
  2. Appropriately sized logo in the backgrounds and slide templates along with applicable logos
  3. Coverage in electronic media as per conference's publicity plan that gets to be in place.
  4. Registration fee will be waived for up to 6 delegates from the sponsor.
Silver 2,500
  1. Appropriately sized logo in the backgrounds and slide templates along with applicable logos.
  2. Coverage in electronic media as per conference's publicity plan that gets to be in place.
  3. Registration fee will be waived for upto 4 delegates from the sponsor.
Bronze 1,500
  1. Appropriately sized logo in the backgrounds and slide templates along with applicable logos.
  2. Coverage in electronic media as per conference's publicity plan that gets to be in place.
  3. Registration fee will be waived for upto 2 delegates from the sponsor.

NOTE:

  • In addition to the above, other negotiable sponsorship is possible on case-to-case basis. Please contact General Chair Sujay Deb (sdeb@iiitd.ac.in) or Sponsorship Chair Subhra Kanti Das (subhra-kanti.das@thalesgroup.com). The objective is to encourage maximum participation and get maximum support from community of scholars.
  • In case of common item of interest, higher sponsorship package sponsors will be given the first preference, if applicable.
  • Logos to be provided by the sponsor as per conference’s specifications; All content for display material, banners, backdrops, advertisements, to be put up as part of sponsorships collaterals, will have to be supplied by the sponsors, in the formats required, within the timelines specified.
  • The payments may be in US $ or Indian National Rupee (INR). With sponsors taking care of and responsible for ensuring prevailing conversion rates – from whatever currency into Indian rupees, on the day of remittance.
