Manuscript Information

Formatting the Manuscripts for IEEE-CS

  • The manuscripts MUST be formatted using the templates provided below:

    1. 8x5x11 2-column Word Template [US Letter]
    2. 8x5x11 2-column LaTeX Template [Be sure to use the template's conference mode.]
    3. LaTeX Instructions
    4. LaTeX Bibliography Files

  • Paper Specifications:

    1. Invited Papers: Up to 8 Pages
    2. Regular Papers: Up to 6 Pages
    3. Short Papers: Up to 4 Pages
    4. Student Research Forum (SRF): Up to 4 Pages
    5. Research Demo Session (RDS): Up to 4 Pages
