Registration for authors

The payment can be made using Indian National Rupee (INR) for the equivalent to US dollar rate mentioned for the different categories below.

$ 1 = 79.80 tentative Rate is applicable as on 1st August 2022

  • Each of the Regular/Short/SRF/RDS papers for the technical program needs to be registered at the respective registration rate by at least one of the authors before (30th November 2022), and be presented at symposium for inclusion in the proceedings as per the IEEE regulations.
  • If an author has multiple papers and he/she is presenting all of them, first paper needs to be registered at the Full rate (Regular/Short). The subsequent papers are allowed to be registered as per the Author rates given below.
  • A regular registration (Regular/Short/SRF/RDS) is mandatory only for a single author who is presenting the paper in the conference. If the co-authors are interested in attending the conference then they are advised to register at the Non Authors/ delegate rate (See participating delegate rate below).
  • The early registration deadline is 30th November 2022. This is also the deadline for all the authors to register.
  • Registration Fee includes breakfast, high tea, conference lunch for 3 days of symposium, dinner on 19th December and buffet on 20th December (only for regular/short/SRF/RDS paper registration and not for WiP). Visit to local historic monuments/places is also covered.
  • It does not include any other expenses like stay, travel etc.
  • The Pre-symposium Tutorials will be free for the Author/Non-Authors registered for the symposium others have to pay a nominal fee as given below.
  • Participants registering for symposium would be entitled to attend whole four days of event (18 December: Pre-symposium Tutorials; 19-21 December: symposium).
  • For more details contact Prof. P. Sreehari Rao or Dr. M. Satish

Email : patri@nitw.ac.in or satishm@nitw.ac.in

Participating delegate: (For co-authors and other interested participants)

  • iSES invites non-presenting co-authors and all other interested to attend the Pre-symposium Tutorials, Symosium, its keynotes, plenary talk and technical sessions by registering at “Non Authors/ delegate” rate.
  • Delegate rate also allows participants who are not presenting paper but interested to witness an IEEE international Symposium and its all-technical sessions including keynotes and plenary talks.
  • It provides opportunity to grow network with international peers and attend 100+ high-quality presentations in 4 days.
  • Single registration participating delegate rate allows a single participant to attend the symposium.

Terms & Conditions

Payment

  • Registration of an additional paper(s) does not include the attendance of co-authors or an extra conference kit. Co-authors may register at a Participating delegate fee.
  • Students are requested to present a valid student ID upon collection of their conference kit on site.

Invoices can be generated beforehand and shared if needed. Please send an email to patri@nitw.ac.in if needed.

Confirmation

  • For online payments, an automated email will be sent to you.
  • Registration will only be confirmed upon receipt of FULL PAYMENT.
  • Please bring along the confirmation letter and present it upon Registration.

Cancellation policy

  • For authors of accepted papers, registration fees can NOT be refunded.
  • Cancellation requests must be in writing and sent by email to Conference Secretary.
  • Registration fee is not refundable after the stipulated dates for whatever reasons, including the failure of obtaining a visa.
