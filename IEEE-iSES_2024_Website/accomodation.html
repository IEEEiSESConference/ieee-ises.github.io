Accomodation

There are several hotels/service apartments in and around 6 km radius of the venue (IIIT-Delhi). Some of them are as below which can be reserved online on your own.

Hotel Crown Plaza


Hotel Crown Plaza is strategically located in Okhla Phase-I, just 20-minute drive from the venue. The hotel features 208 well-equipped guest rooms with complimentary Wi-Fi. Experience the magic of Italian French and Chinese cuisine. Guests can rejuvenate their mind, body and soul at Spazzo, our wellness sanctuary encompassing of a Spa, Fitness Center, Salon & Outdoor Pool.

Approximate Distance from IIIT-Delhi (the venue): 5.9 kms (About 20 mins by Cab)

www.crowneplazanehruplace

Eros Hotel


Nestled in the heart of South Delhi near Nehru Place, Eros Hotel New Delhi epitomizes luxurious 5-star hospitality, blending contemporary design with timeless Old World charm for 25 years. Conveniently located near business hubs like Saket and Nehru Place, and close to shopping, entertainment, and tourist attractions, it is ideal for both business and leisure travelers. Facing the majestic Lotus Temple, the hotel offers opulence amidst urban bustle with state-of-the-art facilities, stunning views, and diverse culinary offerings at its fine dining establishments.

Approximate Distance from IIIT-Delhi (the venue): 4.1 kms (About 15 mins by Cab)

https://www.eroshotels.co.in/

Hotel Shervani, Nehru Place


This is a contemporary, select service hotel, which offers visitors an impressive option in the heart of Nehru Place, Delhi. A unique hotel guaranteed to bring color into the lives of both business and leisure travelers.

Approximate Distance from IIIT-Delhi (the venue): 4.5 kms (About 15 mins by Cab)

https://www.shervanihotels.com/Nehru-Place-new-delhi/

Hotel Sarovar Portico, Nehru Place


This is a contemporary, select service hotel, which offers visitors an impressive option in the heart of Nehru Place, Delhi. A unique hotel guaranteed to bring color into the lives of both business and leisure travelers.

Approximate Distance from IIIT-Delhi (the venue): 4.5 kms (About 15 mins by Cab)

Orien Hotels


Orion Group of Hotels, a distinguished premium hotel chain with 17 exquisite properties in India and abroad, is renowned for exceptional hospitality and unparalleled amenities. Strategically located for convenient access to public transportation and key attractions, Orion Hotels offer luxury accommodation and personalized service, ensuring comfort, convenience, and satisfaction. Our state-of-the-art facilities include high-speed Wi-Fi, comfortable beds, clean linen, and 24/7 room service, all aimed at exceeding guest expectations.

Approximate Distance from IIIT-Delhi (the venue): 4.5 kms (About 15 mins by Cab)

https://theorionhotels.com/

Hotel Pamposh


Located in the vibrant heart of Nehru Enclave Metro Station, The Pamposh caters to both business travelers and couples. Business guests can utilize our cutting-edge business center, ideal for meetings and interviews, while couples can unwind at our rooftop restaurant, enjoying locally sourced cuisine and city views. Our banquet hall is perfect for weddings, receptions, or corporate events with customizable packages and attentive staff. At The Pamposh, we blend business with pleasure, offering comfort, convenience, and charm.

Approximate Distance from IIIT-Delhi (the venue): 5 kms (About 20 mins by Cab)
